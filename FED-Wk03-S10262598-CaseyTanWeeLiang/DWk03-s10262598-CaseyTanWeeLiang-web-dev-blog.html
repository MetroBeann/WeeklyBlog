Week 12 Class

In this week lesson, I understand what's RestDB and NoSQL.
Structured Query Language (SQL) is used to manipulate and retrieve data from structured data format and hold relationships between them.
While NoSQL provides a mechanism for storage and retrieval of unstructured data, it can handle a humongous amount of data and has a dynamic scheme.
It is best fit for unstructured data and works better for larger datasets.
Using restdb.io is simple and helps us to create our own online storage.
